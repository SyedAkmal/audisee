OUR FEATURES

Some of the features that makes a movie experience larger than life.

Full Dynamics

A technique that delivers the full spectrum of sound. Hear the loudest explosion to the softest whisper. A pristine sound delivered from the speakers to the viewer's ears at the precise moment. No sound in the audible sound frequency escapes.

Tonal balance and timbre matching

Tonal balance is the balanced sound at all frequencies during a movie experience. Timbre matching gives you the right sense of the sound during the movie experience. Mismatch in the timbre, caused by an incompatible product or improper acoustic setup.

Optimal bass

The wrong kind of bass emanating from the system can be very unpleasant. The bass from the sub-woofers and speakers is clearer, tight and fast. Resulting in a balanced low frequency sounds without overpowering other frequencies.

Day and Night setting

Matching display type to a room. Do you know that your room light determines which type of display will be appropriate for the room. Black & White levels and color saturation of the picture depends on the type of screen. Plasma, LED, LCD or a projector in relation to the room's ambient light makes or breaks picture quality.

Getting the whole and the right picture. It is common in almost all flat-panel displays for the picture to distort. This is all because of the aspect ratios being wrong. Getting nothing but the picture. In some cases pictures viewed on projectors or flat panels to have many artifacts, which are not a part of the movie. This is when improper black levels add information that the director did not intend to show.
