Acoustics

BROADBAND ABSORBERS

Acoustic broadband absorbers are used to absorb medium to high frequencies. They are suitable for any room lacking corners to help stop and absorb frequencies similar to the corner bass models. Designed to help deaden low, hard to stop frequencies.

Bass traps are acoustic energy absorbers which are designed to damp low frequency sound energy with the goal of attaining a flatter low frequency (LF) room response by reducing LF resonances in rooms

QRD DIFUSER

A QRD diffuser comprises wells of different depths, causing a mixture of phase shifts that diffuse reflected sound. They are used to control reflections in the listening environment, particularly from rear walls and first-reflection points.

POLY CYLINDRAL DIFFUSER

Polycylindrical diffuser and sound absorber merged in a single compact panel. Excellent dispersion characteristics over a very wide frequency range.
