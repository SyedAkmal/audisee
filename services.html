Services

Our technical team develops personalized electronic and acoustic designs for home theatres and music rooms. We offer our consulting services at no extra cost. Audisee installs and calibrates all the equipment to standards and guidelines dictated by authorities in the home entertainment industry.

We are THX, HAA and ISF certified.

PRODUCT INSTALLATION

ISF and THX VIDEO CALIBRATION

Calibration helps make the entertainment experience more natural, this reduces physical and mental fatigue that occur due incorrect audio and video settings Calibrate your audio system for the best audio performance. Calibrate your HDTV flat panel or projector for the best video performance. Calibrate your display device to SMPTE standards. Audisee offers a concession for THX and ISF calibrations. Upon purchases of certain premium products our professional calibration is at no extra cost.

HOME AUTOMATION

Audisee designs full home and Home Theater automation packages. Audisee offers a variety of touch-screen and fixed panel control systems even ones that can be controlled from your smart phone . At Audisee we are proficient at offering cutting edge automation control systems that help in the ease of use of our AV systems making them accessible to all members of the family. Along with AV our automation systems can control HVAC, security and lighting for the entire house.
